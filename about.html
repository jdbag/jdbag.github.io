📖 About Your Dictionary

A free, fast, and beautifully designed dictionary for curious minds

🎯 Our Mission

Your Dictionary was built with one simple goal: to make language accessible to everyone. Whether you are a student writing an essay, a professional drafting an important email, a traveler needing a quick translation, or simply someone who loves words — this tool is for you.

We believe that understanding words is the foundation of understanding the world. Language connects people, cultures, and ideas. A good dictionary should be fast, clear, beautiful, and completely free. That is exactly what we set out to build.

Every feature on this site — from instant definitions to multi-language translation with pronunciation — was designed with real users in mind. No clutter, no paywalls, no unnecessary barriers to learning.

📚 Word Definitions

Your Dictionary provides comprehensive word definitions from a high-quality, open-source database. Every search result includes the word's part of speech, one or more clear definitions, example sentences showing the word in real context, phonetic pronunciation guides, and synonyms and related words.

The interface is designed to present this information cleanly and clearly, so you get exactly what you need without distraction.

🌐 Translation Tool

Our translation feature supports more than 30 languages including Arabic, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Hindi, Persian, Indonesian, Vietnamese, and more.

You can type or paste any text, choose source and target languages, and get a translation instantly. Text-to-speech pronunciation works for both the original and translated output — perfect for language learners and travelers.

🧠 Vocabulary Quiz

The quiz feature tests your knowledge of English words through multiple-choice questions. Each question shows a word and asks you to pick the correct definition from four options. This format is scientifically proven to strengthen vocabulary recall and is widely used in language education.

Your quiz progress is stored locally in your browser so you can track your improvement without needing any account.

❤️ Favorites & History

Save any word you look up to your personal Favorites list for later review. Your saved words and search history are stored privately in your browser — they never leave your device and are never shared with any server or third party.

This makes Your Dictionary an excellent personal vocabulary study companion. Clear your data anytime through your browser settings.

✦ Technology & Data Sources

Free Dictionary API — dictionaryapi.dev

All English word definitions, phonetics, example sentences, and synonyms are sourced from the Free Dictionary API. This is an open-source, community-maintained project that provides comprehensive English language coverage without authentication requirements. It is one of the most widely used free dictionary APIs available.

MyMemory Translation API — mymemory.translated.net

Text translations across 30+ language pairs are powered by the MyMemory API, one of the world's largest collaborative translation memory databases. It combines professional translations with community contributions, making it highly accurate for everyday language pairs including English-Arabic, English-French, and English-Spanish.

Web Speech API — Browser Built-In TTS

Pronunciation is generated using the Web Speech API, which is built into all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. No third-party audio servers are required — speech is synthesized directly on your device, ensuring instant and private playback for any word or translated text.

GitHub Pages — Reliable Free Hosting

Your Dictionary is hosted on GitHub Pages, a free static hosting platform provided by GitHub (owned by Microsoft). It ensures the website is always available, loads quickly from around the world via a global CDN, and requires no server maintenance. The source code is publicly visible at github.com/jdbag/jdbag.github.io.

Browser localStorage — On-Device Storage

All user data — including saved favorites, recent search history, translation history, and quiz scores — is stored using the browser's built-in localStorage feature. This means your personal data is stored only on your own device and is never transmitted to or stored on any external server. You can clear this data at any time from your browser settings.
