📖 About Your Dictionary

Your Dictionary is a clean, simple, and incredibly fast dictionary tool designed to help you expand your vocabulary and understand English words better.

Just type a word on the home screen, and get immediate access to its meaning, usage examples, and related synonyms.

Our mission is to make learning English accessible and enjoyable for everyone, without clutter or distractions.
