📖 About Your Dictionary

A beautiful, fast, and free dictionary app. Find definitions, examples, synonyms, and pronunciations for any English word — instantly.

✦ Features
⚡
Instant Search
Real-time definitions powered by the Free Dictionary API. Results in under a second.
🔊
Pronunciation
Hear how any word is pronounced with native audio playback.
❤️
Favorites
Save words you love and revisit them anytime, stored locally on your device.
🧠
Vocabulary Quiz
Test your knowledge with multiple-choice quizzes built from curated word lists.
✨
Word of the Day
Discover a beautiful new word every day to expand your vocabulary.
📚
Browse Categories
Explore words by topic — Science, Literature, Technology, and more.

Powered by Free Dictionary API

All definitions are sourced from dictionaryapi.dev — a free, open-source API. No signup required, always available.
