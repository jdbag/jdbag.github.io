🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — transparent and simple

Last updated: April 2025

📋 Short version: Your Dictionary is a free tool. We do not collect personal information. We use Google AdSense to show ads and local browser storage to save your preferences. We do not sell your data.

1. About This Website

Your Dictionary (jdbag.github.io) is a free online dictionary and translation service. We provide word definitions, translations between multiple languages, quizzes, and related educational content.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use our website and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location on our servers.

The following data may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Words you have searched recently
  • Words you have saved as favorites
  • Your translation history
  • Quiz progress and scores

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data or cache.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalized ads based on your browsing history. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev)

Word lookup requests are sent to the free public Dictionary API at dictionaryapi.dev. The words you search are transmitted to this API to retrieve definitions. Please review their privacy practices for more information.

Translation API (MyMemory)

Translation requests are sent to the MyMemory API at mymemory.translated.net. Text you submit for translation is transmitted to this API. Please review MyMemory's privacy policy for details on how they handle submitted text.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display text. This may result in requests to Google's servers when you load our pages. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses and browser user agents as part of standard server logs. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

4. Cookies

We do not directly set cookies. However, Google AdSense and other third-party services listed above may place cookies on your device to provide their services, including personalized advertising.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some services on this website.

5. Children's Privacy

Your Dictionary is an educational tool suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this website, please contact us so we can address it.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal security risk related to personal information. Data stored locally in your browser (such as favorites and history) is protected by your browser's built-in security model.

7. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data under laws such as GDPR (Europe) or CCPA (California). Since we do not collect personal data, most of these rights apply primarily to data held by third-party services like Google. You can:

  • Opt out of Google personalized ads via Google Ads Settings
  • Clear your local browser data (favorites, history) at any time
  • Use your browser's privacy or incognito mode to avoid local storage
  • Install browser extensions to block advertising cookies

📬 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us through the GitHub repository for this project.
We will do our best to respond to any inquiries promptly.
