✦ Why Take Vocabulary Quizzes?

🧠 Strengthens Memory

Testing yourself on word meanings is one of the most effective ways to move new words into long-term memory. Research in cognitive science shows that active recall beats passive review every time.

📈 Tracks Progress

Regular quizzing helps you see how your vocabulary is improving over time. Identifying words you consistently miss tells you exactly where to focus your study efforts.

🎯 Improves Reading

A larger vocabulary makes reading faster and more enjoyable. When you recognize words automatically, you can focus on understanding meaning rather than decoding individual words.

🗣️ Boosts Communication

Knowing more words gives you more ways to express yourself clearly. Whether writing or speaking, a strong vocabulary helps you find the right word for every situation.
