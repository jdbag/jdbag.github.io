✦ How to Use Your Favorites

❤️ Save Words You Encounter

Whenever you look up a word on the Home page and find its definition, click the heart icon to save it to your Favorites. Build up your personal word collection one word at a time. The more you save, the more you learn.

🔁 Review Regularly

Come back to this page often to review your saved words. Reading through your list helps reinforce the meanings in your memory. Research shows that reviewing words at regular intervals is the most effective way to retain vocabulary long-term.

🔊 Hear the Pronunciation

Click on any saved word to go to its full definition page, where you can hear it pronounced and see it used in example sentences. Combining reading the definition with hearing the word spoken out loud strengthens your memory significantly.

📝 Write Sentences

For each word in your Favorites, try writing your own sentence using it. This forces your brain to deeply process the word's meaning and how it works in context. Words you use actively stay with you far longer than words you only read passively.

🎯 Quiz Yourself

After building up your Favorites list, head to the Quiz page and test yourself. See how many definitions you can recall from memory. Regular self-testing is one of the most powerful vocabulary learning techniques available.

🌐 Translate Your Words

Take your favorite words to the Translate page and see how they are expressed in other languages. Understanding how different languages handle the same concept deepens your understanding of each word and expands your multilingual vocabulary.
